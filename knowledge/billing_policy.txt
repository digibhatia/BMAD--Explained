Clause 3.1 — Base Charge
The base charge is billed monthly in advance according to the customer's
selected plan.

Clause 4.2 — Late Fee
A late fee of Rs. 50 applies if payment is not received within 15 days of
the bill date.

Clause 2.3 — Tax
GST is applied at 18% on the base plan charge and shown as a separate line
item on every invoice.

Clause 6.1 — Roaming Charges
Roaming charges are billed at the end of the billing cycle in which they
were incurred, and are shown separately from the base charge.

Clause 7.4 — Disputed Charges
Customers may dispute any charge within 30 days of the bill date. Disputed
charges are held (not waived) pending review.
